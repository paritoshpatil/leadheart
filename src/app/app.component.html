The name LeadHeart came about back in 2015 when I had just started to make music and was really drawn to making really sad and gloomy sounding beats. I had gone out for a vacation and I was alone on my way back sitting in a bus reading some fantasy greek-mythology novel when I read the lines “... his heart felt like it was made of lead ...”

And that is how I came up with the name LeadHeart. It is the first name I thought of and I have never looked back. To this day, no matter how much I evolve and grow as a person, the name stays as an integral part of my being. “
